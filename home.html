TCUCEAA: Taguig City University City Educational Assistance Allowance

All TCU students are scholars of the City in so far as their education is paid for by funds from the Taguig City Government coming from the people’s taxes. This is in line with the City Administration’s quest to continue improving access to quality education, enhance completion rate, nurture a culture of excellence among Taguig residents, and ultimately achieve a better and more prosperous life for the people of Taguig.
